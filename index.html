Welcome to Vitaly Tea

Our products and services offer a lot more than you could think of. From printing different kinds of materials and papers, bindings and stitching and even more. Here at Vitaly Tea, we offer all that, plus a cozy nook where you can sit, relax, and wait for your printing job to be finished. And do not forget to grab our delicious mouthwatering drinks too.

Our Services

We offer a complete range of professional printing services including
digital printing, binding, laminating, and cutting.
Whether you're producing business materials or personal projects,
we ensure high-quality output and reliable service every time.

View Services

PRINTING

We offer high-quality digital printing for documents, brochures, posters, and more. Our machines deliver crisp and vibrant prints using premium paper and top-tier ink brands.

BINDING

We provide professional binding options to give your documents a clean and organized finish. Saddle Stitch, Heated Spine, and Wired. We provide a fast and quality binding.

LAMINATING

Protect and enhance your prints with our laminating service. It adds a durable, water-resistant layer that also improves the appearance of your documents, menus, or posters.

CUTTING

We offer precise and clean cutting for your printed materials. Whether standard sizes or custom shapes, we ensure accuracy and a professional finish.
